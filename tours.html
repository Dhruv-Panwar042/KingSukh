Nearby Tours & Attractions

Explore top places near Kingsukh Guest House

Visva-Bharati University

Founded by Rabindranath Tagore, this world-renowned university is a hub of art, culture, and education in Shantiniketan.

Tagore's Ashram

A serene ashram where Rabindranath Tagore lived, wrote, and taught. A must-visit for literature and history lovers.

Kopai River

Enjoy a peaceful stroll or picnic near the scenic Kopai River, often mentioned in Tagore’s writings.

Amar Kutir

A co-operative society offering local handicrafts and souvenirs. Great for shopping and supporting artisans.

Ballavpur Wildlife Sanctuary

Witness deer, birds, and serene nature trails at this nearby wildlife park perfect for family outings.

Shonajhuri Haat

A vibrant open-air market on weekends where tribal music, art, and crafts bring local culture to life.
