About King Sukh Guest House

Tradition, comfort, and culture at one place

Our Concept

King Sukh Guest House is a serene getaway located in Ballavpur, Shantiniketan. We aim to provide warm hospitality with comfortable rooms, delicious Bengali cuisine, and curated local tours for an unforgettable experience.

Nearby Attractions

  • Visva Bharati University: Explore the artistic and academic legacy of Rabindranath Tagore.
  • Amar Kutir: Handicrafts and local art come alive here.
  • Khoai Haat: A lively market surrounded by nature and music.
  • Ballavpur Wildlife Sanctuary: A peaceful escape for nature lovers and birdwatchers.

Food Highlights

  • Shorshe Ilish: Mustard-marinated Hilsa fish delight.
  • Chingri Malai Curry: Coconut cream prawns with rich flavor.
  • Aloo Posto: Poppy seed potato curry – a local classic.
  • Mishti Doi & Rosogolla: End your meal with these sweet Bengali delicacies.
